Peetey

Parakeets are just as smart as Parrots. They are sometimes referred to as 'mini parrots', since Parakeets and Parrots have a lot in common such as inteligence, problem-solving skills and vibrant colors.

  • Scientific Name:: Melopsittacus undulatus
  • Average Length:: 7.1 Inches
  • Average Lifespan: 5 to 10 years
  • Habitat: Tropical climates with grasslands

Parakeets are defined by their short beaks and striped feather formations. Young Parakeets have stripes across their forehead and as they get older, the forehead gets to be a solid color.
